What is x402?

x402 is an open micropayment protocol built on HTTP 402. Send a request, get a payment challenge, sign with your wallet, resend. AI agents can do this automatically with no human in the loop.

FAQ

Which plan should I pick?

Start with Free (no signup). If you need more than 10/day and prefer paying by credit card, go Pro. If you are an AI agent or prefer crypto with no signup, use x402 permissionless.

Can I use both Pro and x402?

Yes. They are independent. Your API key covers Pro quota; x402 USDC payments are separate. Both hit the same endpoints.

What happens when I hit the Pro limit?

Requests return a 402 error. Your quota resets on your next billing date. You can fall back to x402 pay-per-request for extra volume.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Log into Stripe's customer portal (link in your receipt email) and cancel. No fees. Pro access continues until end of the billing period.

What wallets work with x402?

Any wallet that can sign EIP-3009 USDC authorizations on Base. AgentKit (Coinbase), Viem, Wagmi, and most modern wallets. AI agents using CDP SDK work out of the box.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

The Free tier is your trial: 10 scrapes/day forever, no card needed. When you want more, subscribe to Pro.
