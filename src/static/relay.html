FAQ

What exactly does the extension access?
Only the public URLs that anybrowse sends via WebSocket. The extension calls fetch(url, { credentials: 'omit' }) — this means:
  • No cookies are sent with the request
  • No logins or authenticated sessions are used
  • No browsing history or private data is accessed
  • Only publicly accessible pages are fetched
What do I earn?
1 free anybrowse API call per successful relay. Credits accumulate in your account and can be used for any /scrape, /crawl, or /serp request. There's no cap on how much you can earn. Credits never expire.
What kinds of URLs will be sent to my browser?
Only URLs that our normal scraper couldn't access — usually because of Cloudflare protection, LinkedIn, or similar bot-detection. All URLs are safety-checked before relay. Adult, gambling, and other inappropriate categories are always blocked. No authentication tokens or private URLs are ever relayed.
Can I be connected 24/7?
Yes! Leave Chrome open with the extension and you'll passively earn credits. Note: you must be connected for at least 10 minutes before credits start accumulating (to prevent abuse).
Is there a daily limit?
There's a limit of 500 relays per day per relay ID — but this is an extremely high ceiling. Most users will never hit it. Earning is unlimited across days.
How do I use my credits?
Credits are applied to your relay account and used automatically when you make API calls with your relay ID as the billing key. Documentation coming soon. Contact us if you need early access.
Is this safe? Will it slow down my browser?
Yes, it's safe. The extension is a lightweight service worker — it only activates when a relay request comes in and immediately goes back to sleep. Most users see no performance impact. The extension doesn't run any background tabs or inject scripts into pages you visit.
